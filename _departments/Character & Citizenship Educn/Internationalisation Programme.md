journeys with the intent to cultivate leadership and social entrepreneurship
capabilities amongst our pupils to better prepare them to thrive in a fast
changing and highly connected world. Through the immersion programmes both
locally and overseas, the IP aims to broaden pupilsâ€™ knowledge and perspectives
through experiential learning and solution oriented thinking; to encourage
self-learning and self determination; develop social and cultural skill;
encourage understanding and friendship; instill a greater sense of national
identity and pride in our pupils .</p>
