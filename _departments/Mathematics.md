<h4><strong>Our Beliefs</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Every student matters and can achieve a level of mastery of Mathematics
that will serve them well in their lives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teaching and Learning of Mathematics should develop students’ competencies
of thinking critically, decision making and collaborating and communicating
with others.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p>We instill a&nbsp;<strong><em>joy of learning</em></strong>&nbsp;in Math
by orchestrating meaningful learning experiences and classroom discussions
in the classroom that allow students to discover mathematical ideas and
co-construct mathematical knowledge with their peers. With sound mathematical
knowledge, students are guided to adopt a systematic approach through Polya’s
Problem Solving process to solve math problems. Teaching of Math is made
accessible for all students through the use of the Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract
approach that makes the understanding of key Math concepts accessible for
all students.</p>
